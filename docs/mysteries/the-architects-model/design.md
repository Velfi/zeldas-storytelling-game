# The Architect's Model

**Campaign:** One More Question  
**Tradition:** Inverted mystery  
**System focus:** `all_of` campaign gate, scale-model comparison, and staged accident

## Campaign role

This is the locked OMQ capstone. It becomes available only after `The Final Cut`,
`A Private Performance`, and `The Perfect Vintage` are all complete, regardless
of their order. Julian has studied the lieutenant's public methods and prepares
for them, creating escalation without transferring evidence from earlier murders.

The capstone may acknowledge the player's strongest earlier resolution and show
all three completed files in the office. Its murder remains self-contained.

## Premise

The opening shows architect Julian Crest kill engineer Amara Holt in a completed
show apartment, move her to an active construction floor, and stage a fall. He
uses an architectural model to support a false account of the site's barriers.
Julian expects the detective to revisit small discrepancies, invite corrections,
and compare representations with reality; he deliberately resists each habit.

## Cast

- **Amara Holt, victim:** Engineer refusing to certify a dangerously altered tower design.
- **Julian Crest, culprit:** Star architect facing ruin if the alteration is exposed.
- **Nell Avery, suspect:** Site manager hiding an unreported safety failure.
- **Rafi Cole, suspect:** Junior architect who plagiarized part of Amara's design.

## Five investigative locations

Design studio, model room, show apartment, construction floor, and service lift.
The model and full-size spaces are directly comparable.

## Canonical crime

Julian strikes Amara with a stone sample in the show apartment, moves her through
the service lift, and drops her body beside an unfinished edge. He modifies the
scale model to show a barrier that was never installed and cleans the apartment.

## Evidence plan

1. Plaster on Amara belongs to the finished apartment, not the construction floor.
2. Blood remains in a porous stone sample.
3. Service-lift padding carries her coat fibers.
4. Fall geometry does not match the body's landing point.
5. The model's added barrier uses newer adhesive.
6. Current site photographs show no corresponding barrier.
7. Nell's hidden safety report explains her lie and narrows the movement window.
8. Rafi's copied files place him in the studio, away from the body route.
9. Julian refers to the nonexistent barrier as if he personally saw it at the scene.

## Required deductions and finale

Identify the real murder room, demonstrate the service-lift transfer, and expose
the altered model. Julian is asked to mark where he says the body crossed the
barrier, committing to knowledge of a feature that existed only in his model.

## Authoring requirements

The comparison demonstration needs pieces from both world geometry and the
model PoI. The culprit's confidence should visibly erode across three evidence-led
conversations.
The campaign manifest needs an order-independent `all_of` prerequisite. The
locked case appears as a sealed major-crimes file before it opens.
