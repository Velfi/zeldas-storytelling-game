# A Recipe for Silence

**Campaign:** Bellwether Mysteries  
**Tradition:** Community cozy  
**System focus:** Parallel workstations, timers, and innocent rule-breaking

## Campaign role

This middle case unlocks with `The Last Performance at Bellwether Hall` and may
be played in either order. The competition temporarily changes the shared hub,
then leaves a new restaurant stall and revised resident dialogue. Completion
contributes the other requirement for `Death on the Promenade`.

## Premise

Celebrity restaurateur Livia Stone collapses while judging Bellwether's cooking
competition. Poison in her tasting bowl points to a contestant, but the poison
was placed on a judge's spoon during a brief kitchen reset.

## Cast

- **Livia Stone, victim:** Judge planning to reveal the theft of a family recipe and brand.
- **Adrian Moss, culprit:** Sponsor who built his business on that theft.
- **Tessa Bloom, suspect:** Contestant whose dish carried the same herb as the poison.
- **Gabe Noor, suspect:** Kitchen steward who secretly sold reserved ingredients.

## Five investigative locations

Competition floor, prep kitchen, judge's table, pantry, and wash station. One-way
serving doors and station timers make opportunity demonstrable.

## Canonical crime

Adrian coats Livia's engraved tasting spoon with concentrated monkshood in the
wash station, returns it during a reset, and lets her next tasting deliver the
poison. He swaps Tessa's herb jar to direct suspicion. Gabe and Tessa conceal
unrelated breaches of competition rules.

## Evidence plan

1. Poison concentration is greatest on the spoon, not in the dish.
2. Only Livia's engraved spoon has residue beneath its handle collar.
3. Wash-station sanitizer was interrupted during Adrian's sponsor tour.
4. Door timing allows Adrian, but not Tessa, to return the spoon.
5. Adrian's cuff carries the pantry jar's sealing wax.
6. The swapped jar lacks Tessa's usual handwritten date.
7. Tessa tasted her own dish safely from the same pot.
8. Gabe's sales explain the missing inventory without supplying the poison.
9. Adrian identifies the poisoned course before officials announce it.

## Required deductions and finale

Show that the delivery object was the spoon, reconstruct the reset, and explain
the herb-jar frame. Adrian's premature identification of the course, combined
with the wax comparison, is decisive. The final demonstration repeats the
serving path with harmless dye.

## Fairness notes

No chemistry trivia is required: residue distribution and a physical comparison
teach the player the relevant fact.
